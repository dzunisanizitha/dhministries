ABOUT US

Bishop,Prophet RN Makashe

Founder of Deliverance Healing Ministries

Our Bible Verse

"For with God nothing shall be impossible." – Luke 1:37

Our Vision

To boldly proclaim the Gospel to every nation, bringing salvation, healing, and deliverance through the power of the Holy Spirit — setting the captives free and transforming lives worldwide.

Our Mission

To faithfully preach, teach, and prophesy the Word of God empowered by the Holy Spirit; to bring healing, deliverance, and salvation to all who seek; and to equip believers to live victorious, Spirit-filled lives that impact their communities and the nations..

Our Faith Statement

  • We believe in the one true God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe the Bible is God’s inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word.
  • We believe salvation is a free gift from God through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • The Holy Spirit empowers and guides the Church.
  • We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit to transform lives and equip the church for ministry and service.
