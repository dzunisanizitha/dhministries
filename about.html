About Prophet R.N. Makashe

Bishop, Prophet R.N. Makashe is the founder of Deliverance Healing Ministries, a work birthed in 2005 in Mpumalanga Province. What began as a small gathering quickly grew into a dynamic ministry with more than ten branches across South Africa.

He carries a burning passion to see lives transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit. Through revival outreaches, prophetic ministry, healing, and deliverance, he moves under the anointing of God with signs and wonders confirming the Word.
