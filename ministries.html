OUR MINISTRIES

Serving people, serving God

Transforming Lives by the Power of the Holy Spirit

At Deliverance Healing Ministries, we are passionate about transforming lives through the power of the Holy Spirit. We host powerful crusades where freedom is released, healing flows, prophetic voices speak truth, and God’s Word brings life-changing revelation.

Deliverance Ministry

Our Deliverance Ministry focuses on setting people free from spiritual bondage through powerful prayers, prophetic guidance, and the power of Jesus Christ.

Healing Ministry

Our Healing Ministry is committed to restoring physical, emotional, and spiritual health through prayer and the power of God’s Word.

Prophetic Ministry

Our Prophecy Ministry empowers believers through inspired messages from God, guiding the church into His purpose and plan.

Word Ministry

The Word Ministry spreads the transformative power of the Bible through teaching, preaching, and discipleship programs.
