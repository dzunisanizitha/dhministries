Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Deliverance Healing Ministries is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our website or interact with our ministry.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We collect information you provide when donating, partnering, signing up for newsletters, or contacting us, including your name, email, phone number, and payment details.

Automatically Collected: When you visit our site, we may collect your IP address, browser type, and other technical information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to process donations, communicate with you, improve our services, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We only share information with trusted partners necessary to operate the ministry or comply with laws.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information and use secure payment providers for transactions.

5. Your Rights

You have rights under South African law (POPIA) including access, correction, and deletion of your personal data. Contact us at info@dhministries.org to exercise your rights.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please email us at info@dhministries.org or call +27 83 476 3071.
