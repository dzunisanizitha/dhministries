Privacy Commitment

Last updated: October 2025

At Deliverance Healing Ministries, we honour the trust you place in us. This page explains how we collect, manage, and protect your personal information when you interact with our ministry online, visit events, or use our services.

1. Personal Data We Receive

Information you provide: Name, email, phone, and donation details when you communicate with us or contribute to our ministry.

Technical and usage data: Your IP address, browser type, device, and activity on our website to ensure optimal service and analytics.

2. Purpose of Collecting Data

Your information helps us to:

  • Process donations securely.
  • Provide updates on ministry activities, events, and spiritual resources.
  • Enhance your experience on our website and other platforms.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including POPIA.

3. Sharing Information

We never sell your information. Data may only be shared with trusted service providers to facilitate donations, email communications, or legal compliance.

4. Data Protection Measures

We use appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with South African law (POPIA), you have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@deliverancehealingministries.org.

6. Use of Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, analyze website traffic, and provide a personalized experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

7. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please contact our Data Steward via info@deliverancehealingministries.org or call +27 83 476 3037.

8. Commitment to Minors

Our ministry is dedicated to protecting the privacy of children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without parental consent.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
