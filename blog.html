Heritage Conference 2025 – Miracles, Healing, and Prophetic Ministry Across South Africa

Powerful Move of God in Limpopo

From Tzaneen to Kgapane, the Heritage Conference 2025 attracted attendees from Limpopo, Mpumalanga, Gauteng, and beyond. Under the anointing of Prophet R.N Makashe, countless miracles occurred. People received healing from chronic illnesses, experienced deliverance from spiritual bondage, and encountered prophetic insights that transformed lives.

Vibrant Ministry Activities

The conference featured worship sessions, prophetic teachings, prayer for healing, and ministry workshops. Attendees testified of personal breakthroughs, restored hope, and miraculous encounters. God’s presence was tangible, impacting both first-time visitors and longtime members.

Miracles and Testimonies

Many shared testimonies of instant healing, financial breakthroughs, and divine direction. The prophetic messages delivered by Prophet R.N Makashe provided clarity, encouragement, and spiritual empowerment. Attendees from all corners of South Africa were united in faith, worship, and the supernatural.

Breaking Evil Pot Sunday Service in Limpopo

On Sunday, 06 September 2025, Deliverance Healing Ministries in Limpopo, South Africa experienced a powerful move of God during the Breaking Evil Pot Service. Led by Prophet R.N Makashe, the atmosphere was filled with deliverance, worship, and restoration as chains were broken and lives were set free...

Breaking Evil Pot Sunday Service in Limpopo – Chains Broken Across South Africa

Biblical Foundation – Judges 6:25–28

The service was anchored in Judges 6:25–28, where God commanded Gideon to destroy the altar of Baal and build a new altar for the Lord. Inspired by this scripture, Prophet R.N Makashe held an evil pot symbolizing spiritual bondage. When he broke it, it prophetically signified the breaking of chains and the restoration of lives across Limpopo and South Africa.

Powerful Move of God

As the pot shattered, the congregation erupted in prayer and worship. Many testified of instant breakthroughs and spiritual freedom. The power of God moved so strongly that people shared their deliverance stories not only in Limpopo but also with families across South Africa.

Testimonies of Breakthrough

Child Secures Permanent Job in Just Days

A woman brought a photo of her child on her phone to Prophet R.N Makashe during the service. Her child had struggled for years with temporary jobs. After the Prophet prayed over the photo, within just a few days, the child signed three contracts and is now employed permanently. God truly broke the chains of delay and opened doors for this young life!

Sibling Finds Job in Tzaneen, Limpopo

The same woman also testified about her sibling, a lawyer, who had never met Prophet R.N Makashe personally. After her encounter with the Prophet for prayer, God opened doors, and the sibling secured a job in Tzaneen, Limpopo. This powerful testimony shows that God’s favor works across distance and obstacles.

Healing from Multiple Diseases

On 15 April 2023, a 59-year-old woman from Atteridgeville, Pretoria shared her powerful testimony. She had been suffering for years from multiple health challenges including high blood pressure, diabetes (sugar), and womb cancer. She had never truly enjoyed her teenage or adult life due to these long-term illnesses.

Journey to Deliverance Healing Ministries

Determined to receive a breakthrough, she traveled all the way to Limpopo to meet with Prophet R.N Makashe at Deliverance Healing Ministries. During the service, the Prophet prophesied over her life and prayed for her healing. At the time, her sugar levels were critically high, and her blood pressure reached 220. She was being closely monitored by medical staff while sleeping, with checks every hour.

Miraculous Results

After returning home and visiting her doctors following the prophetic prayer, she experienced miraculous healing. Her sugar levels normalized, her blood pressure stabilized, and her overall health dramatically improved. This powerful testimony is a reminder of God’s unlimited power and favor through Prophet R.N Makashe and Deliverance Healing Ministries in Limpopo!
